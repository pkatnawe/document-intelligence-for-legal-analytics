Still genuinely open

Deferred on purpose, to settle with real data rather than guess: whether the document mix is clean enough for OCR-first (test on their docs), and the exact confidence thresholds (tune once extractions accumulate — see Evaluation).

⚖️ Sensitive data & confidentiality — flagged, not building yet. Legal documents carry PII and privileged information, so a real engagement must decide what may be sent to which model at all — a question for the firm's experts, not us to assume. Open threads: which content is AI-eligible vs human-only; whether to redact PII before any model call; and model placement — our tier-1 gemini-3-5-flash is a global, cross-geo endpoint (data leaves the region) while tier-2 claude-opus-4-8 sits inside the Databricks perimeter, so sensitive documents would favour the in-perimeter model. We're not implementing this for the POC, but the design leaves room (Unity Catalog governance + a redaction step before extraction).
